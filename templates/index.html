About RKR Logistic

Courier management is a complex process that involves the efficient movement and storage of goods. This includes not only products, but also materials, tools, equipment, and even people. Key aspects of courier management include transportation, packaging, warehousing, materials handling, and inventory management.

One of the most important aspects of courier management is tracking. With advanced tracking systems, you can know the exact location of your goods at any time. This real-time information is crucial for managing the supply chain effectively, allowing for adjustments to be made as needed.

In the context of manufacturing, courier management ensures that each machine and workstation receives the necessary resources when they are needed. This is particularly important in a constantly changing environment, where it's often necessary to adjust the flow of materials to prevent both shortages and over-supply. With effective courier tracking, you can maintain real-time oversight of your supply chain, making adjustments as necessary to ensure smooth operations.
